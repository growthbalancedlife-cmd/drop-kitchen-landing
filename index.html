The D.R.O.P Kitchen Story

D.R.O.P stands for Diverse Recipes Outstanding Palate — a philosophy that culinary excellence draws from everywhere, honors every tradition, and elevates every guest to the center of the experience.

Founded in Minneapolis by chef and creative Monty B, D.R.O.P Kitchen blends classical technique with international influence, bringing restaurant-quality craft directly to your table — wherever that table is.

Food isn't just sustenance here. It's ceremony. It's community. It's care made edible.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

D.R.O.P Kitchen is based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. Local and regional events are prioritized. Travel fees may apply for events outside the Twin Cities metro area — inquire for details.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, allergen-aware, and culturally specific dietary needs are all considered during menu design. Please specify any restrictions during your consultation.

Is there a minimum guest count for catering?

Minimum guest counts vary by service type. Contact us directly for specifics around your event type and size.

How far in advance should I book?

For private dining, 1–2 weeks advance notice is recommended. For larger events and catering, 3–6 weeks is ideal. Last-minute requests are considered based on availability.

Do you offer meal prep services?

Weekly meal prep availability is limited. If this is something you're interested in, include it in your inquiry and we'll discuss what's possible.
